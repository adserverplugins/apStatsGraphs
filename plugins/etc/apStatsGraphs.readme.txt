
apStatsGraphs
=============
OpenX Ad Server has on-screen reports that display the statistics for
advertisers, campaigns, websites, and zones, and an overall report that shows
"global history". This free plugin adds a new item to the Statistics section,
that displays the global history statistics in the form of a drill-down graph.
